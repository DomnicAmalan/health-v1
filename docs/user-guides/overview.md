# User Guides Overview

Health V1 is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to serve multiple clinical and administrative roles within a healthcare organization. Each role interacts with the system through tailored workflows, dashboards, and tools that match their day-to-day responsibilities.

This section contains dedicated guides for each role, covering the specific workflows, screens, and features relevant to their work.

## Roles

### Doctor

Patient management, clinical documentation using SOAP notes, ordering labs, imaging, and medications, and reviewing results. Doctors operate primarily through the clinical encounter workflow, from patient check-in through assessment and plan creation to checkout.

[Go to Doctor Guide](./doctor/overview)

### Nurse

Vital signs recording during triage, patient assessment, and medication administration. Nurses play a central role in the encounter workflow, handling initial patient intake and ongoing care tasks.

[Go to Nurse Guide](./nurse/overview)

### Front Desk

Appointment scheduling, patient check-in, and new patient registration. Front desk staff manage the daily schedule, verify demographics and insurance, and handle the OPD queue. They are also responsible for duplicate patient detection and record merging.

[Go to Front Desk Guide](./front-desk/overview)

### Admin

User and role management, workflow configuration, compliance monitoring, and RustyVault secrets management. Administrators configure the system at the organization level, define custom workflows and state machines, and ensure HIPAA compliance through audit trail review.

[Go to Admin Guide](./admin/overview)

### Lab Tech

Specimen collection queue management, result entry with automatic abnormal flagging, and result verification before release to ordering providers. Lab technicians ensure that laboratory workflows run accurately and efficiently.

[Go to Lab Tech Guide](./lab-tech/overview)

### Pharmacist

Prescription dispensing, pharmacy order verification (drug interactions, allergy cross-reference, dosage checks), and medication inventory management. Pharmacists serve as a safety checkpoint in the medication workflow.

[Go to Pharmacist Guide](./pharmacist/overview)

### Billing

Invoice creation from completed encounters, payment recording with multiple payment methods, and insurance claims processing. Billing staff ensure financial accuracy, GST compliance, and proper reconciliation.

[Go to Billing Guide](./billing/overview)

### Patient

Patient portal features including viewing appointments, accessing lab results, requesting prescription refills, and secure messaging with providers. Patient-facing features are planned for future releases.

[Go to Patient Guide](./patient/overview)
